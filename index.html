About Us

The mission of Global English Labs is to tie our core values into coaching English Language Learners (ELL) to become not only proficient speakers of English but better communicators in general, as well as concientious global citizens.

Global English Labs is our English proficiency program wherein we teach English to learners at all levels. Global English Lab is built upon five core values:

  • altruism
  • purpose
  • oneness
  • individual autonomy
  • competent communication

This website contains resources that tie these core values together with English language learning.

We are hopeful that these resources will prove helpful in aiding others in realizing their own personal missions toward mastery of English.

We have a deep appreciation for English in particular and for language and communication in general. We invite you to join us.
