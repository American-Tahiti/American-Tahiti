About Me

My personal mission is to tie my core values into coaching English Language Learners (ELL) to become not only proficient speakers of English but better communicators in general, as well as concientious global citizens.

Global Language Lab, my English language learning laboratory wherein I teach English to willing subjects, is built upon five core values:

  • altruism
  • purpose
  • oneness
  • individual autonomy
  • competent communication

This website contains resources that tie these core values together with English language learning.

I am hopeful that these resources will prove helpful in aiding others in realizing their own personal missions, including mastery of English.

I have a deep appreciation for English in particular and for language and communication in general. Additionally, I am an experienced teacher of English to language learners from all corners of the globe.
